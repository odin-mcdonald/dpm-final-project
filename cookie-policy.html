What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences and to improve your browsing experience. Cookies may also be used to track your activity on a website for analytical purposes.

What Cookies Do I Use?

I use Google Analytics cookies to collect and analyze data about your use of my portfolio website. This helps me understand how visitors interact with the website and identify areas for improvement. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data, which are stored on your device and used to track your activity on the website.

How Can You Control Cookies?

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies or to delete cookies that have already been set. However, please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect your browsing experience on my website.
